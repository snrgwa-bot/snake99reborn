PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

Snake 99 Reborn ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

🔒 Quick Summary: We collect minimal data. Your game progress is stored locally on your device. We don't sell your personal information to anyone.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Data: High scores, highest level reached, lives count, and game settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms.
  • Game Center Data: If you choose to use Game Center, Apple collects and manages your Game Center profile, scores, and achievements according to Apple's privacy policy.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of your purchase but do not have access to your payment details.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media
  • Browsing history
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

How We Use Your Information

The information stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress, high scores, and level progress
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide a personalized gaming experience
  • Display your scores on Game Center leaderboards (if you opt in)
  • Process and verify in-app purchases

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults). We do not transmit your personal game data to external servers. Your data stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, general location data (coarse), and ad interaction data to serve ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center: For leaderboards and achievements. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Advertising

Our App displays banner advertisements powered by Google AdMob. These ads appear at the bottom of the screen during gameplay. The ad network may collect:

  • Device identifiers for ad serving
  • Coarse location data
  • Ad interaction and performance data
  • Diagnostic information

We do not use App Tracking Transparency (ATT) to request access to your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). Ads are served using contextual signals and Apple's SKAdNetwork for attribution.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App does not require any personal information to play.

Data Retention

Your game data is stored on your device until you:

  • Delete the App
  • Reset your statistics within the App settings
  • Reset your device

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View your game statistics in the App's Settings
  • Delete your data: Reset statistics in Settings or uninstall the App
  • Opt-out of Game Center: Sign out of Game Center on your device
  • Avoid ads: Purchase Unlimited Lives or simply don't tap "Watch Ad"

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from Apple's built-in iOS security features, including device encryption.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Notifying you within the App for significant changes
